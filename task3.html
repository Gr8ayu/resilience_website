Task 3

Task Description:

Welcome to the third round of resilience. Since you have created a wonderful product and successfully marketed it, Now let us take you to a situation where your company has to deal with one of the following situations:
  1. The world has just witnessed the outbreak of corona virus, and it has been found that the region your business is situated in, is the epicenter of the virus i.e. Wuhan, China. The situation is getting worse with each passing day and is severely affecting the trade. How are you going to deal with the following situation? (Timeline: Coronavirus has just been discovered and is spreading rapidly).
    • Identify how your business would be affected in the pandemic.
    • Analyze how your target customers would be affected.
    • Examine if you can benefit from the situation, if yes then how? If any sort of modifications in your product can reap you benefits, then mention them as well.
    • Analyze if it would be appropriate to shift your business from its current location and mention potential markets, their pros and cons etc.
    • Analyze all possible aspects of finance in the whole scenario, for example: Costs involved, Overheads etc.

  2. The world has just witnessed the outbreak of corona virus. Your company is highly dependent on the imports from China, but now the trades are being rapidly cut off from them. So, now how are you going to deal with this situation?
    • Identify how your business would be affected in the pandemic.
    • Examine if you can benefit from the situation, if yes then how? If any sort of modifications in your product can reap you benefits, then mention them as well.
    • Mention the materials that you might be importing from china.
    • Mention the potential alternatives
    • Select the appropriate alternative with reasons (include overheads as well)
    • Make a rough estimation of the cost involved.
    • Project your estimated profit or loss that you might incur after this.

What You Need to Do:

Choose any one of the above scenario and make a detailed analysis on the points mentioned in the description. Submissions can be in the form of report, presentation or any other mode of your preference.

Judging Criteria:

Adding realistic dimensions would give you an edge.
  1. Market Research
  2. Creativity and innovation
  3. Professionalism of report
  4. Feasibility of marketing plan

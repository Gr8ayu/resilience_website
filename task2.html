Task 2

Task Description:

Welcome to the second round of the Resilience. The heart of your business success lies in its marketing. Most aspects of your business depend on successful marketing. The overall marketing umbrella covers advertising, public relations, promotions and sales. Marketing is a process by which a product or service is introduced and promoted to potential customers. This task will test your marketing skills by evaluating the research done on the previously modified product that you made during the first task (Hack-It). This task gives a proper insight of different aspects of marketing which includes doing market research to finding the potential customers and improve the market reach of your chosen brand or product.

What You Need to Do:

  • Choose the product you used in task Hack-IT.
  • Project your marketing strategy in the most innovative manner.
  • A detailed report of the Marketing Plan for the product is to be created. Report should include:
    • Analysis of the current marketing plan of the original unmodified product.
    • Market Survey used to decide the modification to the product.
    • New target customer segments (minimum 2), not reached by the original product.
    • Marketing strategy for each new segment identified by you.
    • Cost involved in the entire marketing campaign.
Additions to the report can be made by you so as to aid your plan and to project it in a better way.

General rules :

  1. The marketing plan should be made assuming real life scenario.
  2. Each target segment must have a proper dedicated marketing plan.
  3. New target segments should be different from the customer base of the original product.
  4. Points will be given on how well the market analysis is done and the feasibility of the new strategies.
  5. Any plagiarism will be penalized accordingly.

Judging Criteria:

Adding realistic dimensions would give you an edge.
  1. Market Research
  2. Creativity and innovation
  3. Professionalism of report
  4. Feasibility of marketing plan

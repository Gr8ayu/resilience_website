Preliminary Task

Task Description:

Create and maintain a Instagram page with your team name and use your creativity to design a fitting profile picture and cover page. Sell your team and its ideals in the most innovative and imaginative way possible.

What You Need to Do:

  • Regularly update the page with posts about Resilience task submissions or any other entrepreneurship related posts.
  • Share all the posts of Entrepreneurship Cell, RVCE.
  • Increase engagement on your page. The goal is to get as many likes and shares as possible and increases the reach of your page throughout the course of the competition.

General rules :

  • Only one page per team.
  • Regularly update your page with relevant and engaging content.
  • Include mandatory hash tags #Resilience #ECellRVCE and/or any other relevant hash tags.
  • We will ask you to submit a report of the page at the end of the final round. Your page will be evaluated at the end of every round.

Judging Criteria:

Your team will be judged on the following criteria. Do keep them in mind while creating your page.
  • No. of likes on the post.
  • Quality of posts
  • Regularity of posts
  • Use of hash tags
  • Creativity and imagination

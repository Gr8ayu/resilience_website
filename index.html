  • FAQs
  • What is the competition about?
    It is an entrepreneurship competition which assesses your team building and creativity abilities based on a series of tasks.
  • Is this an ideation competition?
    No, this is not an ideation competition. You needn’t have an idea planned prior to the competition. The competition explores the other facets of entrepreneurship.
  • What is the number of members in a team?
    There can be three to four members on a team.
  • What is the duration of the competition?
    The competition spans over a period of a month and a half.

  • FAQs
  • Is it necessary for the team members to be from the same college?
    No, the members of the teams can be from different colleges too. There is no restriction to stick to one college to get team members.
  • Is there a limit on how many teams can register from a college/city?
    No. There is no restriction on number of teams.
  • Is the competition only open to undergraduates?
    No. There is no limitation on age, institute or city. It is a pan-India competition open to all age groups.
  • How can we monitor our progress?
    Teams will be provided with access to links where the leaderboard will be updated regularly.
